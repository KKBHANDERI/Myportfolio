Overview

Pawll is a pet care app that offers a single, convenient platform for managing all pet-related needs. From finding nearby sitters and trainers to learning about pet events and nutrition, Pawll helps pet owners stay informed and connected—anytime, anywhere.

What is the Problem?

Pet owners often struggle to find trustworthy information and services in one place. They’re forced to juggle multiple apps and websites to locate sitters, vets, food options, or local events—wasting time and creating confusion.

Project Goal

Pawll simplifies the pet care experience by offering a one-stop app tailored to each user’s profile. It auto-filters services like sitters, trainers, and vets, and includes features like chat support and event listings—making pet parenting easier and more efficient.

Logo variations

Pawll Logo

The PAWLL logo is designed to visually express trust, care, and convenience—core values of a modern pet service platform.

Typography & colour pallet

Poppins is a modern, geometric sans-serif typeface that was carefully chosen to complement and reflect the essence of the brand. Its clean structure and balanced proportions contribute to a seamless and user-friendly interface. As a neo-grotesk workhorse, it ensures readability, consistency, and excellent performance across all digital platforms.
