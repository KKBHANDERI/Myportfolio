First Client Logo That Sparked a Career

As a new designer just starting out, this was the first logo I ever created that got officially approved by a client. It was designed during my internship, and it became the face of a brand-new supplements company based in Windsor, Ontario.

Seeing something I designed used in the real world — on packaging, social media, and marketing — felt unreal. This project not only gave me confidence, but also opened the door to a full job offer, which made me realize the real impact design can have in a business.
